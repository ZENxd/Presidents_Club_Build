THE AWARD

The 2015 President’s Club will be at the Kahala Resort & Hotel in Oahu March 1-5, 2016

The President’s Club is a special event recognizing top performance in sales by honoring the top 3-4% of all sales quota carrying employees. The CEO and executive staff hosts the winners and one guest for a special 5 days in Hawaii where the winner will be recognized in several events as well as many special activities.

SELECTION PROCESS

The 2015 Nominations will begin November 3 and be open through November 17th. At the end of day 11/17/2015 pacific time, the nominations will close and then region sales management will review and pass on their selections by November 20th to the Global Sales Managers – Danilo Cazzola and Frederic Laget. They will select the final winner’s for the respective sales organizations by November 25th. Mike McMullen CEO will review and then announce the 2015 President’s Club winners in early December.




ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Eligible employees must be in the sales value chain and be considered quota carrying personnel. This includes employees in Sales, Sales Management (operating/integrating level) and Application Resources. In 2015, there will a total of 42 winners selected – 33 from LSAG/ACG Sales and 9 from DGG Sales. Eligible employees should exceed expectations in the following areas:

  • Sales Results
    A consistent track record of Over Quota Achievement. Winner must be at least 100% of target quota. Consistent results in gainintg market share in their assigned territory.
  • Sales & Planning Skills
    Forecasting, territory management, sales process and resource management.
  • Customer Relationship and Loyalty
    Skillfully manages and establishes the needed customer relationships driving preference and loyalty for Agilent.
  • One Agilent Behavior
    Excellence in teamwork and disciplined collaboration.
  • Leadership/Role Model
    Demonstrated leader in the sales organization coaching and helping team members.
